Stan Lee

The man who walked in with pure IMAGINATION.

Here's a time line of Stan Lee's life:

  • December 28, 1922 - Born to Celia Lieber and Jack Lieber in New York City.
  • 1939 - Had several jobs in his life. One of then was that the was an office assistant at Timely Comics.
  • 1940 -became the Editor of Timely Comics.
  • 1961 - Noteworthy comic creations of Stan Lee, the Fantastic Four.
  • 1962 - Created Spider-Man.
  • 1963 - Created the X-men.
  • 1942 - Became the editorial director and publisher.
  • 1999 - Formed Stan Lee Media.
  • 2001 - Started POW! Entertainment.
  • 2002 - Published his autobiography, filed a lawsuit against Marvel for not receiving any profits from the Spider-Man movie.
  • 2008 - Got a National Medal of Arts.
  • 2011 - Got the Star on the Walk of Fame for Motion Picture award.
  • 2012 - Announced his new YouTube channel, ‘Stan Lee's World of Heroes’, airing various programs created by him , at San Diego Comic-Con International.
  • 2015 - Wrote the book, ‘Zodiac’, along with Stuart Moore
  • 2005 - Got married to Joan Clayton Boocock and lived on Long Island for the first few years of their marriage. The couple has a daughter, Joan Celia ‘J.C.’ Lee and Joan had a boy, Jan Lee, who died in infancy.
  • November 12, 2018 - dies at the age of 95.

"“With great power comes great responsibility.”."

-- S.Lee
